Don Pepe's Taqueria

Don Pepe's Taqueria

If you've ever ordered a burrito at Taco Bell, just go ahead and forget about those right now. If you go to Chipotle's for burritos, I would similarly advise you to consider going to Don Pepe's Taqueria, which is locally owned, instead. Don Pepe's Super Burritos are massive, filling, and delicious. I first "discovered" Don Pepe's on DoorDash in October 2020. My brother and I had a DoorDash rotation of Teriyaki Don, Bonchon, and Colorado Grill going, and I really needed to find another restaurant to order from. I settled on Don Pepe's, as it had good reviews, and it is now a regular part of the rotation. Unfortunately, my brother doesn't like it much, so on days when I order from there, he will usually make some other lunch, microwave a frozen pizza, or pick up food from somewhere else. His loss!

My Favorite Super Burritos at Don Pepe's

Carne Asada Super Burrito

The Carne Asada Super Burrito has beans, rice, cheese, guacamole, and of course, a generous amount of carne asada. While it's really hard for me to pick one Super Burrito from Don Pepe's to call my favorite, I would probably consider the carne asada one to be a narrow favorite, although it is slightly more expensive than the carnitas burrito.

Carnitas Super Burrito

The Carnitas Super Burrito has all of the same ingredients the Carne Asada Super Burrito has, minus the carne asada. It's my other favorite menu item to order at Don Pepe's. It's very common for me to not be able to decide which burrito I want, so I will often order both and pick one of them at random to eat for lunch, and the other to eat for dinner!
