My Early Life

I was born in Downey, California (a suburb of Los Angeles) one score and some change ago. I attended Rio Hondo Elementary for kindergarten through grade 5. My family lived there for about 11 years before we moved to Fresno, where I've lived ever since. I attended Jane Addams Elementary for 6th grade before applying to attend the magnet school Computech Middle School, where I was accepted. I had always grown up using computers, as we had at least one desktop computer in our house since I was 6 or 7 years old, but Computech was where I really started to learn about computers. That was the first time where I had an actual computer lab as a full class. We learned simple programming (in a programming language that I quite honestly couldn't tell you the name of even if I tried!) We also played Flash games online a lot, whenever we had free time available between assignments.
