My Favorite Splash Cafe Items

Clam Chowder

Splash Cafe is without a doubt well-known for their clam chowder, and a lot of people probably go there just for that! I'm certainly no exception to that rule, as I will always get at least a Bowl (if not a Pint!) of Clam Chowder when my girlfriend and I go.

Calamari and Chips

My second favorite thing to order at Splash Cafe! While clam chowder is a slam dunk to order every time I go, my second item has varied from time to time. Three of the times I went, I would get Calamari and Chips. The other time, I got a Fish Sandwich.
