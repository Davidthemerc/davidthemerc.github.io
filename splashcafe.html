Splash Cafe

Splash Cafe - Pismo Beach

My girlfriend introduced me to the Splash Cafe on our first trip to Pismo Beach, where went went for Valentine's Day in 2020. We were participating in the 2020 Pismo Beach Wine Walk, which is held around Valentine's Day each year. We walked from venue to venue across Pismo Beach, sampling food, snacks, and various wines. One of our stops was the Splash Cafe, which offered wine and a free cup of clam chowder to all participants. This was the first time I'd ever actually had clam chowder before, and from the first taste I was hooked! We make it a point to drive down to Pismo Beach at least twice a year (when the weather is nice) to get some clam chowder and enjoy the coastal breeze. Althoug the lines for Splash Cafe are always very long, they do luckily take orders over the phone and online for quick pickup at their side window. The only downside to this is that parking is metered and very limited, but we've only had to park further away on one occasion. If you do go there and park at the meters for pickup, be sure to actually pay, as I've seen Pismo Beach police ticketing non-paying cars at that exact spot multiple times! We have never gotten a ticket, because I'm always very cautious and pay for meters when it's required! If you are ever in San Luis Obispo, they also have a location!

My Favorite Splash Cafe Items

Clam Chowder

Splash Cafe is without a doubt well-known for their clam chowder, and a lot of people probably go there just for that! I'm certainly no exception to that rule, as I will always get at least a Bowl (if not a Pint!) of Clam Chowder when my girlfriend and I go. They also sell frozen chowder which you can have shipped to your house, if traveling to Pismo isn't an option!

Calamari and Chips

My second favorite thing to order at Splash Cafe! While clam chowder is a slam dunk to order every time I go, my second item has varied from time to time. Three of the times I went, I ordered Calamari and Chips. The other time, I got a Fish Sandwich.
