My Teenage Years

After promoting past Computech Middle School, I attended Edison High School, which is actually adjacent to Computech. Middle school students and high school students actually take the same buses (terrible idea, I know!) and get dropped off at a central location to head off their separate ways. My high school years were easily the years where I struggled the most academically (more than any year in college), because as I mention in my About Me profile on the main website page, I simply never felt engaged with my classes. The only thing I disliked more than my classes was the lunch hour rush. If your class wasn't located near the Cafeteria, and you didn't make a beeline for it while sprinting as fast as you could, you would be waiting in line forever. Most of the time, even when I ran as fast as I could, I could never get there early enough to beat the rush. My classes were never near the Cafeteria. Sigh...
