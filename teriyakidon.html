Teriyaki Don

My dad and I were out on the town doing something (probably shopping at his favorite store, the 99 Cents Only Store, I can't really say for sure), when we decided that we were going to eat out before we went home. He had heard about this teriyaki restaurant, Teriyaki Don, from a coworker who recommended it to him. It sounds good to me, so we drove over to Cedar & Herndon where it's located. They specialize in donburis, which are Japanese-style rice bowl dishes, which at Teriyaki Don, consist of rice, a small salad, and your choice of meat. They have quite a few choices of donburi, but I usually stick to two: the NY Steak Donburi (medium rare), and the Spicy Crispy Chicken Donburi.
