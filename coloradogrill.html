Colorado Grill

Colorado Grill

I actually knew about Colorado Grill for several years before I actually set foot inside to actually try the place. I had seen commercials for it on TV a few times, and would drive by it a few times on my way back from attending Fresno State Men's Basketball games, but most of the time, it was already late at night, and they were already closed. It became sort of a running joke between my dad and I, as we would drive by it so often but never actually go there. So, one evening a couple years ago, about 5:30ish, I told my dad that we should finally go to Colorado Grill. We were going to a 7 PM tipoff for a game, but Colorado Grill is only about a mile from the Save Mart Center, so there was plenty of time to go there and eat first. Ever since then, we try to go at least 7-8 times a season (out of an average 18 game season) to eat before or after the game. No more driving past Colorado Grill!

My Favorite Menu Items at Colorado Grill

The Boulder Burger

The Boulder Burger is my go-to order at Colorado Grill. It's a 1/2 lb. patty topped with mayo, mustard, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and for me, an extra addon of mushrooms. The first time I ordered a Boulder Burger, I started eating it and it was just so good! I ate about half of it before I even realized that I had forgotten to order it with cheese! If you know me, almost 100% of the time, without fail, I will order my burgers with cheese. That is probably the closest thing I have to an absolute rule in my life; burgers must have cheese! The Boulder Burger was the first burger I'd ever had where I didn't miss the cheese at all. It's so delicious that it easily stands on its own. To this day, I never need to order cheese with my Boulder Burger.

The Chocolate Malt

Although I'm normally a milkshake person, if a restaurant has a malt available on their menu, I will usually take it over a milkshake. Although they are a good bit thicker than milkshakes, Colorado Grill provides a massive straw that helps counteract some of that problem. The richness of a malt and how it brings out the flavors of the ice cream will usually sell me on ordering a malt over a milkshake 90% of the time.
