My College Years

My College Years

A few months before the end of my last year in high school, Fresno City College came to Edison High and held an enrollment event. I actually found out about it the day of, entirely by chance, as I hadn't given much thought to what I was going to do after I graduated high school. My first period art teacher had evidently heard about it, and knowing that I was a senior, pulled me aside and asked if I had heard about it. I had not, and realizing that since I really didn't have a better plan at that point in time, asked to be dismissed from class so I could go check it out. I went to the cafeteria, where the event was set up, and was able to register for a few courses that day.

I attended Fresno City for 3 years, taking my time to earn my units to transfer to Fresno State. Fresno City was where I really started to mature and take my classes seriously. I was lucky in how I picked my schedules each semester, as I never felt like I had a bad instructor or a class where I never felt engaged. I was able to rack up well over a 3.0 GPA (I even got on the Dean's List a semester or two), more than good enough to transfer to Fresno State. I transferred in the fall of 2011, and attended Fresno State for two years, graduating with a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science in May 2013.
