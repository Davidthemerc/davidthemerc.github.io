Global Energy Corporation Policies

  • We do not sell less than one (1) unit of fuel to customers, NO EXCEPTIONS.
  • All fuel purchases must be made in whole units (to simplify auditing procedures.)
  • Do not price fuel at $100 or more (we're already anti-competitive enough and can't afford more scrutiny!)
  • If a customer cannot afford the amount of fuel they need, the system will round down to the amount of fuel they can afford.
  • If a customer wants to use the car wash, and they can afford to use it, ALWAYS offer to send their vehicle through the car wash.
  • Do not, under any circumstances attempt to edit the customer's funds in the transaction system. Our fraud prevention system is running 24/7.
  • Do not, under any circumstances allow someone without enough money for at least one gallon of fuel to attempt to fuel up.
  • Do not, under any circumstances send away a potential paying customer before providing all services.
  • Failure to follow these policies will result in a written violation.
  • Three violations, and you're fired! There's more where you came from!
  • Global Energy Corporation reserves the right to change its prices at any time, for any reason.
