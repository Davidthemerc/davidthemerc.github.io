Global Energy Corp. Policies

  • We do not sell less than one (1) unit of fuel to customers, NO EXCEPTIONS.
  • Three mistakes with customers, and you're fired! There's more where you came from!
  • All fuel purchases must be made in whole units (to simplify auditing procedures.)
  • Do not price fuel at $100 or more (we're already anti-competitive enough!)
  • If a customer cannot afford the amount of fuel they need, the payment system will round down to the amount of fuel they can afford.
  • Do not, under any circumstances attempt to edit the payment/transaction system to allow a customer to fuel up. We are watching.
  • Do not, under any circumstances send away a potential paying customer, or allow someone without enough money to attempt to fuel up.
  • We reserve the right to change our prices at any time, for any reason.
